Energy Conservation

Fairbanks Energy Services is a full-service design/build energy conservation firm dedicated to providing cost-effective retrofit solutions for our clients. Our team has over 30 years of experience in providing energy conservation services for commercial and industrial clients throughout the country. Our comprehensive approach and deep knowledge of federal, state and municipal incentive programs allows us to identify, develop and install solutions that maximize savings while minimizing capital outlay.

Financing

Fairbanks Energy Services has the experience and knowledge to arrange financing of efficiency projects that will meet client requirements.As part of every efficiency project development, FES will qualify efficiency measures for the highest level of utility incentives and then accept the utility incentive as partial direct payment from the Utility.

Energy Audit

An Energy Audit is an initial analysis of all facility systems by an experienced energy conservation specialist. Over the course of an hour, different components of the facility are examined and available personnel are interviewed to understand how the facilities systems are being utilized.
